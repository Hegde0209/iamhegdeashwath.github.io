Blackboard Transact

August 2015 - May 2016
Project: International Cloud Platform
Role: Software Developer
Description:

International Cloud Platform is a cloud based product to universities and schools across the globe where students with a single campus ID - card or mobile based web-application give their students access to everything from vending machines to financial aid to a safe trip across campus. And students seamless and cashless transactions which mean a greater efficiencies and reduced operational costs. This is a Angular based portal for students to maintain their transactions made on their cards. This application is also localized based on the browsers accept-language, language stored in the database or the user-preferred language.
